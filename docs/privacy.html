Privacy Policy — Last Updated: June 6, 2026

Skriv is a simple, local-first plain text editor for Android. We believe in complete transparency and user privacy. Our policy is straightforward: we do not collect, store, or share your personal data or document contents.

1. Personal Information Collection

Skriv does not require you to create an account, log in, or register to use the application. We do not collect, store, or have access to any personal identifying information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or device identifiers.

2. Document and File Storage

Skriv operates strictly as a local-first editor.

  • All documents you open, edit, or create remain stored entirely on your device or in your personal cloud storage (such as Google Drive or OneDrive) through Android's Storage Access Framework.
  • Your document contents are never uploaded to any external servers by Skriv.
  • The application does not maintain an internal database of your document text. The only locally saved data is a list of recently opened file paths (URIs) and your editor preferences (such as font size and dark mode), which are stored securely on your device's private app storage.

3. Internet Access and Connectivity

Skriv does not request or use the Android INTERNET permission. The application is completely offline and incapable of transmitting any data over the network.

4. Third-Party Services

Skriv does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics frameworks (such as Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics), tracking tools, or advertising networks. Your activities inside the app are entirely private to you.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. However, our core principle of collecting zero data will remain unchanged.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out to the developer via the support channels listed on the Google Play Store listing.
